Appointment app -The perfect booking system 
 
The Appointment App is based on a scheduling and booking system that allows users to book 
appointments with one or multiple providers based on real-time availability. The application 
supports flexible scheduling rules, slot management, capacity control, and end-to-end 
appointment lifecycle management.
